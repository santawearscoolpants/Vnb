
VNBWAY Community Promotion Program

VNBWAY is preparing to launch a Community Promotion Program, an initiative designed to allow individuals to participate in building the brand while earning rewards for promoting it. This program is not only a marketing initiative. It is also a community empowerment model that gives young people, students, creators, and entrepreneurs an opportunity to generate income and gain real-world marketing experience.

Participants who join the program will receive tools to promote the brand and will be rewarded whenever their promotion results in verified purchases via a unique code.

The program will open soon. Interested individuals are encouraged to join the waitlist to receive early access.
Purpose of the Program

The Community Promotion Program was created with three objectives:

    To expand the reach of the VNBWAY brand through authentic word-of-mouth promotion.
    To create economic opportunities for individuals who want to earn through promotion and referrals without needing capital.
    To support the wider community by encouraging youth participation in entrepreneurship, marketing, and digital commerce.

How the Program Will Work

Once the program launches, individuals who register will receive a unique referral code linked to their account.

Participants can then share the brand with their networks through:

    Social media
    Personal recommendations
    Events and community gatherings
    Digital platforms

Whenever someone makes a purchase using the participant’s referral code, the system records the sale and assigns rewards to the promoter.
Commission Structure

Participants will earn commissions for every successful purchase made through their referral code.

    Standard commission: 10% of every successful sale
    Special support commission: 15% commission for physically challenged participants

This structure is designed to ensure that the program remains inclusive and supportive of individuals who may face employment barriers. Commissions will be paid bi-weekly. Affiliate marketers are known as “VNB Stewards.”
Additional Rewards

Beyond commission payments, participants will also have access to additional incentives. These include:

    Store Credits: Participants may earn credits that can be used to purchase VNBWAY products.
    Milestone Recognition: Participants who reach specific promotion milestones will be recognized by the brand.
    Free Products: Participants who generate consistent sales may receive complimentary products as appreciation for their contribution.

These incentives are designed to reward both performance and long-term participation.
Affiliate Milestones

To encourage consistent engagement, the program will include milestone rewards. Examples may include:

    For every 5 successful purchases through your referral code in a week: Recognition within the VNBWAY community
    For 10 successful purchases: Eligibility for product rewards
    20 successful purchases: Access to exclusive brand merchandise

Higher milestones may unlock ambassador opportunities and additional recognition. The exact structure will be announced when the program officially launches.
Community and Youth Empowerment

One of the core motivations behind this initiative is youth empowerment. Many young people have access to social networks but lack access to economic opportunities.

The Community Promotion Program allows participants to:

    Learn how product promotion works
    Develop marketing and communication skills
    Earn income through legitimate referral systems
    Build connections within a growing brand ecosystem

The program is intended to function as both an earning opportunity and a learning experience, so each individual will take our sales and marketing course from industry experts.
Program Guidelines

To maintain the integrity of the brand and the fairness of the system, participants will be expected to follow basic guidelines:

    Participants must promote the brand honestly and responsibly.
    Misleading advertising, spam promotion, or misrepresentation of the brand will not be permitted.
    The brand reserves the right to review referral activity and remove participants who violate these standards.
    These guidelines help ensure that the program remains credible and beneficial for everyone involved.

Who Should Join the Waitlist

This program may be especially valuable for:

    Students
    Physically challenged individuals
    Young entrepreneurs
    Content creators
    Community leaders
    Individuals interested in marketing and brand promotion
    Anyone looking for an additional income stream through referrals
    No upfront payment is required to participate.

What Happens After Joining the Waitlist

Individuals who join the waitlist will:

    Receive early information about the program launch
    Be notified when registration officially opens
    Gain priority access to secure referral codes

The first participants may also receive early program benefits depending on launch capacity.
